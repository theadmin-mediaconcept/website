FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the type and complexity. Logo designs typically take 3–5 days, websites 2–4 weeks, and brand identity packages 7–10 days. We'll give you a specific timeline when you contact us about your project.

How do I pay for services?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and other convenient payment methods. We typically require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the balance due upon project completion.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All our services include revision rounds as specified in the service description. We work closely with you to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

Can you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Absolutely! We work with clients across Africa and internationally. All collaboration is done remotely through email, WhatsApp, and video calls, making it easy to work together regardless of location.

Will I own the rights to my designs?

Yes! Upon full payment, you receive 100% ownership and commercial rights to all designs and deliverables we create for you. You'll receive all source files and formats.

What information do I need to provide to get started?

To get started, we'll need a brief about your business, your target audience, any existing brand materials, examples of styles you like, and your budget. The more detail you provide, the better we can serve you.
