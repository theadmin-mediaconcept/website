Meet Our Founder

The creative mind and driving force behind The Admin Media Concept's success.

Emmanuel Ayomide

Founder & Creative Director

Emmanuel Ayomide is a passionate creative professional with over 5 years of experience in graphic design, digital marketing, and brand development. His journey began with a simple belief: that great design has the power to transform businesses and create meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of modern design trends, Emmanuel has helped over 200 clients across various industries establish their brand presence and achieve their business goals. His expertise spans from logo design and brand identity to web development and digital marketing strategies.

Emmanuel's approach to design is rooted in understanding each client's unique story and translating that into compelling visual narratives. He believes that effective design goes beyond aesthetics – it should communicate, engage, and inspire action.

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works. My goal is to create designs that not only look beautiful but also serve a purpose and drive results for my clients."

- Emmanuel Ayomide

When he's not creating stunning designs, Emmanuel enjoys exploring new creative techniques, staying updated with industry trends, and mentoring aspiring designers. He is committed to continuous learning and innovation, ensuring that The Admin Media Concept remains at the forefront of creative excellence.
